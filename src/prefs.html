URI utilities

Relations and linked items

Features built around Zotero’s related-items relations (dc:relation) and the linked-items mechanism.

Supported markdown: **bold**, *italic*, ~~strike~~, `code`, [label](url).

Filters

Controls that hide, dim, or scope the visible set: the items-tree filter pane (the toolbar ▾ button) and the per-library / file-type filters in the tabs menu.

Bookmarks

Bookmarks across two scopes: a Bookmarks dropdown on the collections-pane toolbar for items / collections / searches, and a Bookmarks tab in the reader sidebar for in-document locations.

Note: Bookmarks don’t sync across computers at the moment — they’re stored locally in <Zotero data dir>/weavero/bookmarks.json. (Zotero’s plugin-managed synced storage is in development; this will switch over once it ships.)
